Modern Scroll

Quiet luxury, with a hint of magic

This template gives you a calm, negative‑space layout with a soft turquoise mist and gold accents. Try the gold symbols in the header to switch sections — content will momentarily disperse into mist and then reform.

Gallery preview

Imagine your images here. On transition, the current content dissolves slightly, and the background mist deepens for a moment for a cinematic feel.

About this style

Colors are easy to change (see :root at the top). You can turn off motion at the bottom-right toggle. The “mist warp” filter is used only during section switches to keep things smooth.
